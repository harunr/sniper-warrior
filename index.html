ABOUT THE INSTRUCTOR

Tofiel Ahamed

Tofiel Ahamed is a 3rd generation Jacksonville native and delighted to be a part of this great community. He is a true security expert with 25 years of experience in the se-curity industry. He has appeared on FOX, CBS, ABC & NBC news outlets. He has been Featured in numerous publica-tions and has trained thousands to protect themselves and their homes from thugs and thieves. He is certified by both the NRA & the USCCA as a firearms instructor. He is also an NRA Range Safety Officer and Defensive Pistol Instructor. He often says: "My students are like family so I take their security personal. I want them to feel confident that they can protect themselves and their families!"
