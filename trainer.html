ABOUT THE INSTRUCTOR

Tofiel Ahamed

Tofiel Ahamed is a 3rd generation Jacksonville native and delighted to be a part of this great community. He is a true security expert with 25 years of experience in the security industry. He has appeared on FOX, CBS, ABC & NBC news outlets. He has been Featured in numerous publications and has trained thousands to protect themselves and their homes from thugs and thieves. He is certified by both the NRA & the USCCA as a firearms instructor. He is also an NRA Range Safety Officer and Defensive Pistol Instructor. He often says: "My students are like family so I take their security personal. I want them to feel confident that they can protect themselves and their families!"

Tactical Concealed Carry is a radically different concealed carry class focusing on the TRUTH about how "Real Attacks" take place. The Concealed Carry Class goes over way more than just the boring, lecture-style, gun shop curriculum into real strategies, tactics and training suggestions to help you prepare! You have to experience it to believe it! We also offer The Warrior Academy for those who are looking for a Fully Immersive, Reality Based training environment that prepares you to defend against the worst threats you may face! In The Academy, we utilize high tech, military/law enforcement grade simulators to take training to a level never before seen in the world of concealed carry.
